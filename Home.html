SAF is an interfaculty sports event organized at Universiti Tun Hussein Onn Malaysia (UTHM) to encourage students with talents in sports. SAF is dedicated to fostering a healthy and active lifestyle. Next, SAF also fosters teamwork, discipline, and leadership through various sports and athletic activities. Moreover, our mission is to provide opportunities for students to showcase their athletic potential, represent the faculty in inter-faculty competitions, and build a community that values physical fitness and camaraderie. Whether you're a seasoned athlete or just starting your fitness journey, SAF offers an inclusive environment for everyone to participate, grow, and thrive. Together, let's elevate sports excellence and nurture a strong spirit of unity and sportsmanship within UTHM students.
